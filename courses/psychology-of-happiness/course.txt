# FINAL COURSE SELECTION: "The Psychology of Happiness"

## Course Overview
A practical and evidence-based course on what truly makes people happy. You'll explore insights from leading psychologists, neuroscientists, and philosophers on fulfillment, meaning, and emotional well-being. Through expert interviews, cutting-edge research, and practical strategies, you'll learn how happiness can be cultivated — not just pursued — across work, relationships, and everyday life. Each episode features credentialed researchers and practitioners sharing evidence-based science from the world's leading institutions.
