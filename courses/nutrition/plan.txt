# Nutrition Course Plan
## "Nutrition — Eating well, finding peak health, and the gut-brain connection simplified"

### Course Overview
This course will guide listeners through the science of nutrition, gut health, and the fascinating gut-brain connection. We'll progress from foundational concepts to cutting-edge research, featuring interviews with leading researchers, clinicians, and scientists.

### Course Progression Strategy

**BEGINNER LEVEL: Foundations of Nutrition & Gut Health**
- Goal: Make complex nutrition science accessible and engaging
- Topics:
  - Basic nutrition principles and healthy eating
  - Introduction to the gut microbiome
  - How food affects energy, mood, and health
  - Simple, actionable dietary strategies
  - Understanding probiotics and prebiotics
- Format: Well-known hosts interviewing top experts
- Ideal guests: MDs, PhDs, nutrition researchers with strong communication skills

**INTERMEDIATE LEVEL: Deep Dive into Gut Science & Metabolism**
- Goal: Build technical understanding of how nutrition works
- Topics:
  - Gut microbiome science and diversity
  - Gut-brain axis and mental health connection
  - Metabolic health and insulin sensitivity
  - Dietary approaches (Mediterranean, plant-based, etc.)
  - Inflammation and chronic disease
  - Personalized nutrition concepts
- Format: Subject-specific podcasts with domain experts
- Ideal guests: Microbiome researchers, metabolic health scientists, clinical researchers

**ADVANCED LEVEL: Research Frontiers & Optimization**
- Goal: Cutting-edge insights for optimization and longevity
- Topics:
  - Advanced microbiome research and interventions
  - Longevity nutrition strategies
  - Precision/personalized nutrition
  - Nutrigenomics and epigenetics
  - Time-restricted eating and fasting
  - Emerging research on specific nutrients/compounds
- Format: Expert-to-expert technical discussions
- Ideal guests: Leading researchers, pioneers in the field, published scientists

### Quality Criteria

**MUST HAVE:**
✅ Identifiable expert guest with clear credentials
✅ Interview format (not solo host monologue)
✅ Scientific/evidence-based approach
✅ Specific expertise in nutrition, gut health, or related field
✅ From reputable podcast (Top 200 or respected subject-specific show)

**RED FLAGS (Auto-reject):**
❌ No guest credentials mentioned
❌ Host monologue format
❌ Spiritual/mystical approach to nutrition science
❌ Promoting specific products without scientific backing
❌ "Guru" approach without credentials
❌ Wikipedia or general knowledge as primary source
❌ Casual conversation format without expertise
