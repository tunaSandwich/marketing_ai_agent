# STORYTELLING & SPEAKING COURSE
## "From nerves to narrative—how to move a room"

This course takes learners on a comprehensive journey from overcoming the fear of public speaking to mastering the art of storytelling and presentation. Each episode features expert interviews with world-class speakers, storytelling champions, communication professors, and professional coaches who have mastered the craft of moving audiences.

The course is structured to build confidence and skills progressively:
- BEGINNER: Overcoming fear, building confidence, learning story fundamentals
- INTERMEDIATE: Mastering techniques, narrative structure, business communication
- ADVANCED: Rhetoric, world-class performance, executive presence

All 18 episodes feature expert guests being interviewed, representing maximum podcast diversity (18 different podcasts), and covering the full spectrum of storytelling and speaking expertise.
