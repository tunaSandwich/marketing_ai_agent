===================
COURSE OVERVIEW
===================

This course will guide listeners through the journey of career reinvention, from understanding what drives meaningful work to executing major career pivots. The course emphasizes learning from people who've actually made successful transitions and experts who study career development.

===================
LEVEL BREAKDOWN
===================

BEGINNER LEVEL: Understanding Career Reinvention
- Introduction to why people pivot and what makes work meaningful
- Finding purpose, values, and strengths
- Stories of successful career changes from well-known figures
- Overcoming fear and psychological barriers
- The mindset shift required for reinvention
- Understanding different models of career fulfillment

TARGET: Accessible interviews with founders, authors, or researchers explaining career transitions to general audiences. Focus on inspiring stories and foundational concepts.

INTERMEDIATE LEVEL: The Mechanics of Career Transition
- Practical strategies for making career changes
- Building new skills, credentials, and expertise
- Networking, personal branding, and positioning
- Financial planning and risk management
- Managing identity shifts and impostor syndrome
- Navigating in-between phases
- Industry-switching strategies

TARGET: Deeper conversations with career coaches, founders who pivoted, executives who switched fields, or experts on professional development.

ADVANCED LEVEL: Mastering Career Resilience and Purpose
- Portfolio careers and multiple income streams
- Purpose-driven entrepreneurship and impact work
- Future of work trends and adaptability
- Long-term career architecture
- Building resilience and antifragility
- Creating sustainable, meaningful work over decades
- Research on career satisfaction and fulfillment

TARGET: Cutting-edge discussions with researchers, thought leaders, serial entrepreneurs, or pioneers in career design.
