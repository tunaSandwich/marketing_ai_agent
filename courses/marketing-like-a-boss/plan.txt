# MARKETING LIKE A BOSS - COURSE PLAN
## Psychology, Virality, and Growth in the Modern World

---

## COURSE OVERVIEW

**Title:** Marketing Like a Boss — Psychology, Virality, and Growth in the Modern World
**Slug:** marketing-like-a-boss
**Target Audience:** Marketers, entrepreneurs, founders, brand builders, and growth professionals
**Core Goal:** Learn from marketing experts, psychologists, CMOs, and successful founders who've mastered the psychology of persuasion, viral growth mechanics, and modern marketing strategies

---

## COURSE STRUCTURE

### Beginner Level: Marketing Fundamentals and Psychology
**Focus:** Introduction to marketing psychology, consumer behavior, storytelling basics, brand building fundamentals
**Guest Types:** Well-known marketers on accessible podcasts (Lex Fridman, Tim Ferriss, How I Built This)
**Topics:**
- Psychology of persuasion (why people say yes)
- Brand storytelling fundamentals
- Consumer behavior basics
- Why content goes viral
- Building trust and authenticity
- Marketing psychology 101
**Listener Outcome:** Understand what makes marketing work, why people buy, and the foundational principles of persuasion
**Format:** Well-known hosts (Lex, Tim Ferriss, Guy Raz) interviewing marketing experts or psychologists

### Intermediate Level: Tactical Marketing Execution
**Focus:** Growth hacking, viral mechanics, content strategies, modern channels (social media, podcasts, email), community building, conversion optimization
**Guest Types:** CMOs, growth leaders, brand builders from successful companies (Lenny's, a16z, 20VC, Marketing podcasts)
**Topics:**
- Growth hacking tactics
- Building viral loops
- Content that converts
- Community-led growth
- Social media strategy that works
- Conversion optimization
**Listener Outcome:** Learn practical frameworks for building viral loops, optimizing funnels, creating content that spreads, and driving measurable growth
**Format:** Marketing podcasts or business shows with tactical discussions

### Advanced Level: Marketing at Scale
**Focus:** Brand positioning, market category creation, network effects in marketing, platform dynamics, marketplace of attention, behavioral economics, the future of marketing
**Guest Types:** Marketing thought leaders, behavioral economists, platform builders, serial CMOs (Acquired, specialized podcasts)
**Topics:**
- Category design (creating new market categories)
- Network effects in marketing
- Advanced behavioral economics
- Brand at scale
- The attention economy
- Future of marketing (AI, new platforms)
**Listener Outcome:** Master advanced marketing patterns, psychological triggers, platform strategies, and long-term brand building
**Format:** Technical discussions with professors, senior CMOs, marketing scientists
