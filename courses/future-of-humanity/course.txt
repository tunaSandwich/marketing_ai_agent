## COURSE PHILOSOPHY

This course explores humanity's long-term future through four interconnected lenses:
1. **Longevity & Human Enhancement** - Can we extend healthspan and transcend biological limits?
2. **Artificial Intelligence & Existential Risk** - Will AI save us or threaten our existence?
3. **Space Exploration & Cosmology** - Are we destined to become a multi-planetary species?
4. **Civilization & Long-term Thinking** - How do we ensure humanity thrives for millennia?

**Learning Progression:**
- **Beginner**: Accessible introductions from world-class experts explaining why these topics matter
- **Intermediate**: Deeper mechanisms, challenges, and policy implications
- **Advanced**: Research frontiers, philosophical debates, and unresolved questions

**Why These Episodes:**
Every episode features a verified expert (professor, MD, PhD, or demonstrated authority) in interview format. No solo monologues, no Wikipedia explanations. Only genuine expertise from people actively shaping humanity's future.
