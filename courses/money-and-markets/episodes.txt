# Money & Markets — Economics and the forces that drive prosperity, inflation, and power
## Episode Evaluation

## Evaluation Criteria Applied

⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT: Nobel laureates, top university professors (MIT, Harvard, Stanford, Princeton, etc.), Former Fed officials, Chief Economists at major institutions, Published authors with bestselling economics books, Top-tier podcast with verified expert guest

⭐⭐ GOOD: Economics professors at solid universities, Central bank economists, Think tank researchers (NBER, Brookings, Peterson), Economic journalists at major publications with expertise, Policy advisors with clear credentials

⭐ ACCEPTABLE: Legitimate economist with credentials, Policy role but not top-tier, Relevant expertise but not ideal positioning

❌ REJECT: No identifiable credentials, Host monologue without expert guest, Ideological content without empirical basis, Amateur podcast, Spiritual/mystical interpretations, Wikipedia-based explanations
