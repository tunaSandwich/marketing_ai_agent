## COURSE SUMMARY

This 18-episode course progresses from accessible neuroscience fundamentals through treatment mechanisms to cutting-edge research and policy implications:

**Beginner**: Top podcast hosts interviewing world-class experts who explain brain mechanisms clearly (Lembke, Maté, Volkow, Lewis, Hari, Brewer)

**Intermediate**: Deeper dive into mechanisms, co-occurring conditions, treatment modalities, and different theoretical frameworks (Pham on PTSD/dual diagnosis, Engelbrecht on MAT, Maté trauma deep-dive, Peele on alternative addiction models, Tatarsky on harm reduction, neuroscience education)

**Advanced**: Research frontiers including psychedelics (Garcia-Romeu), challenging conventional thinking (Hart x2), comprehensive neuropharmacology (Malenka), genetics (Camden Initiative), and policy (Szalavitz/Wen)

**Podcast Diversity**: 18 different podcasts spanning Top 200 shows (Huberman Lab, Armchair Expert, Rich Roll), respected medical podcasts (Cracking Addiction), academic content, and policy discussions.

**Expertise**: Every episode features MD, PhD, or equivalent expert credentials from institutions including Stanford, Columbia, Johns Hopkins, Harvard, UC Davis, NIDA, and others.
