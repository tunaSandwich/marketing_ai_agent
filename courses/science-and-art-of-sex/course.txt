FINAL COURSE SELECTION: The Science (and Art) of Sex
====================================================

COURSE OVERVIEW:
This course provides a candid, educational exploration of sex through the lenses of biology,
psychology, and connection. Featuring experts in sexual health, relationships, and neuroscience,
the course unpacks desire, communication, anatomy, and pleasure with equal attention to science
and empathy. Designed to demystify sexuality, reduce shame, and foster healthier, more satisfying
relationships for every stage of life.

The 18 episodes feature expert interviews from board-certified physicians, licensed therapists,
PhDs, and specialized practitioners—ensuring every episode delivers credible, science-backed
information in an accessible, shame-free format.
