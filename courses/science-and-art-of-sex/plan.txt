COURSE PLAN: The Science (and Art) of Sex
==========================================

COURSE DESCRIPTION:
A candid, educational exploration of sex through the lenses of biology, psychology, and connection.
Experts in sexual health, relationships, and neuroscience unpack desire, communication, anatomy, and
pleasure — with equal attention to science and empathy. This course helps demystify sexuality, reduce
shame, and foster healthier, more satisfying relationships for every stage of life.

FOUNDATIONAL CONCEPTS (BEGINNER):
----------------------------------
- Basic anatomy and how bodies work
- Understanding desire and attraction (biological/psychological)
- Communication basics for intimacy
- Consent fundamentals
- Normalizing conversations about sex
- Body positivity and reducing shame

CORE MECHANICS/SYSTEMS (INTERMEDIATE):
-------------------------------------
- Pleasure science and orgasm physiology
- Pelvic floor health and sexual function
- Hormone impacts on sexuality across lifespan
- Sexual dysfunction (causes and treatments)
- Relationship dynamics and maintaining intimacy
- Communication techniques for couples
- Different approaches to sexual health

RESEARCH FRONTIERS/DEBATES (ADVANCED):
-------------------------------------
- Neuroscience of desire and attachment
- Menopause and sexual health research
- Advanced sexual medicine and treatments
- Gender and sexuality research
- Cutting-edge approaches to sexual dysfunction
- Hormone replacement therapy debates
- Cultural and societal impacts on sexuality
