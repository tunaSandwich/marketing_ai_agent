# THE ART OF NEGOTIATION - COURSE STRATEGY

## Course Purpose
A practical course on the psychology and tactics of effective negotiation, drawn from FBI hostage negotiators, business dealmakers, and behavioral scientists. Listeners will learn how to read people, create value, handle difficult conversations, and reach agreements that last. Each episode adds tools—from emotional intelligence to game theory—that apply whether you're buying a car, asking for a raise, or resolving conflict.

## Course Philosophy

### Episode Selection Hierarchy
1. **Expert being interviewed** - FBI agents, Harvard professors, behavioral economists, organizational psychologists
2. **Practitioners sharing insights** - Authors, negotiation consultants, deal-makers
3. **Academic researchers** - Professors, scientists with published work

### Progression Philosophy by Level

**BEGINNER (6 episodes):**
- Format: Accessible hosts interviewing world-class negotiation experts
- Goal: Learn fundamental tactics through engaging stories and practical examples
- Examples: Lex Fridman/Joe Rogan interviewing Chris Voss, Tim Ferriss interviewing William Ury
- Focus: FBI tactics, Getting to Yes principles, body language, influence psychology
- Credentials: Look for FBI experience, Harvard affiliations, bestselling authors

**INTERMEDIATE (6 episodes):**
- Format: Subject-focused podcasts with domain experts discussing deeper tactics
- Goal: Build sophisticated understanding of behavioral economics, cognitive biases, and advanced techniques
- Examples: Behavioral economics podcasts, organizational psychology discussions
- Focus: Decision-making psychology, cognitive biases, difficult conversations, salary negotiation
- Credentials: PhD, professor titles, organizational psychology backgrounds

**ADVANCED (6 episodes):**
- Format: Expert-to-expert conversations on complex negotiation scenarios
- Goal: Cutting-edge insights on game theory, high-stakes deals, cultural nuances
- Examples: VC deal-making, international negotiations, research frontiers
- Focus: Game theory, venture capital negotiations, emotional intelligence mastery
- Credentials: Published researchers, VCs, international negotiation experts
