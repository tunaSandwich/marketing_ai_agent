## EVALUATION METHODOLOGY NOTES

**Quality Assessment Process:**
1. Read episode description thoroughly
2. Identify guest name and role
3. Look for specific credentials (fund managed, AUM, track record, years, firm)
4. Verify interview format (guest name + "interview with" or "discusses with")
5. Assess level based on complexity of topics discussed
6. Cross-reference with plan.txt expertise indicators
7. Apply conservative standards (when in doubt, rate lower)

**Red Flags Applied:**
- ‚ùå No guest name in description
- ‚ùå Host says "I explain" or "I discuss"
- ‚ùå No fund or institution mentioned
- ‚ùå Generic "expert" without specific role
- ‚ùå Podcast appears amateur or low-production
- ‚ùå Focus on get-rich-quick, day trading, or speculation
- ‚ùå Technical analysis only without fundamentals
- ‚ùå Crypto-only focus without traditional investing foundation
