# FINAL COURSE SELECTION: "Philosophy Fundamentals"

## Course Overview
A journey through humanity's deepest questions — what we can know, what's right, and what it means to live well. You'll meet the great thinkers from Plato to contemporary philosophers and unpack key branches like metaphysics, epistemology, ethics, political philosophy, and philosophy of mind. The course concludes with modern ideas like posthumanism, AI consciousness, and the simulation hypothesis, making philosophy feel alive and relevant today.
