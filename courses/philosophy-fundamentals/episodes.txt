EPISODE: David Chalmers: The Hard Problem of Consciousness
Episode ID: 791930
Podcast ID: 22625
Podcast: Lex Fridman Podcast
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT
Level: BEGINNER

Guest: David Chalmers
Credentials: Professor of Philosophy and Neural Science at NYU, co-director of NYU's Center for Mind, Brain, and Consciousness. Coined "the hard problem of consciousness."

Expertise Signals:
- Professor at major university (NYU)
- Director of consciousness research center
- Coined influential philosophical term
- Lex Fridman interview (Top 200 podcast)

Why This Episode:
Perfect introduction to philosophy of mind. Chalmers makes the hard problem accessible while maintaining rigor. Discusses consciousness, simulation hypothesis, meaning of life, free will, and philosophical zombies - all key intro topics.

What Learners Will Gain:
- Understanding of what consciousness is
- The "hard problem" concept
- Why consciousness matters philosophically
- Connection to AI and technology
