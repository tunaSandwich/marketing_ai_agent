## EVALUATION CRITERIA

Using the course quality standards from plan.txt and CLAUDE.md:

**⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT** - Use these first:
- Multiple strong credentials (e.g., "Professor at Cambridge and author of...")
- From Top 200 podcast OR respected topic-specific show
- Clear interview format with expert being interviewed
- Guest has done significant work in the field
- Specific expertise signals documented in description

**⭐⭐ GOOD** - Solid backup:
- One strong credential clearly stated
- Reputable podcast (recognizable name or topic-specific)
- Interview format confirmed
- Relevant expertise even if not top-tier

**⭐ ACCEPTABLE** - Use only if needed:
- Some expertise mentioned but vague
- Less known podcast but seems legitimate
- Relevant but not ideal

**❌ REJECT** - Never use:
- Hits any red flag (storytelling, solo monologue, no credentials)
- No identifiable expert
- Wrong format
- Off-topic or tangential
