MEDITATION & MINDFULNESS COURSE - EPISODE EVALUATION
===================================================

This document contains detailed evaluations of all episodes found during the comprehensive search process. Each episode is rated using the quality scoring system and includes specific expertise signals identified.

EVALUATION CRITERIA:
⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT - Multiple strong credentials, reputable podcast, clear interview format
⭐⭐ GOOD - One strong credential, reputable podcast, interview format confirmed
⭐ ACCEPTABLE - Some expertise mentioned but vague
❌ REJECT - Hits red flags, no identifiable expert credentials
