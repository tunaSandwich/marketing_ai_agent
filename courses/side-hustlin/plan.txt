## 2. Course Strategy by Level

### **BEGINNER LEVEL: Finding Your Path**
**Core Questions to Answer:**
- What is a side hustle and why start one?
- How do I find the right idea for ME?
- What mindset shifts are needed?
- How do I validate an idea before investing time/money?
- What are common beginner mistakes?

**Ideal Episode Types:**
- Well-known founders telling their origin stories (How I Built This style)
- Accessible interviews with entrepreneurs who started small
- Authors of popular business books on Tim Ferriss or similar
- Practical, encouraging, story-driven content
- Focus on psychology, motivation, and getting started

**Learning Journey:**
1. Introduction & mindset (why side hustle, overcoming fear)
2. Idea generation & opportunity recognition
3. Market validation basics
4. First steps & minimum viable experiments
5. Time management & resource allocation
6. Learning from failure stories

---

### **INTERMEDIATE LEVEL: Launch & Iterate**
**Core Questions to Answer:**
- How do I actually launch and get my first customers?
- What marketing strategies work for bootstrapped ventures?
- How do I price my product/service?
- How do I build audience and traction?
- When should I pivot vs. persevere?
- How do I balance day job with side hustle?

**Ideal Episode Types:**
- Founders discussing launch strategies and early growth
- Marketing experts with track records (not generic advice)
- Product managers or growth leaders from successful companies
- Investors sharing patterns from portfolio companies
- Domain-specific experts (e-commerce, SaaS, content, etc.)

**Learning Journey:**
1. Launch strategies & go-to-market
2. Finding first customers & early marketing
3. Pricing psychology & strategy
4. Building audience (content, social, community)
5. Product iteration based on feedback
6. Balancing full-time work with side hustle growth

---

### **ADVANCED LEVEL: Scale & Systematize**
**Core Questions to Answer:**
- How do I scale beyond myself?
- What systems enable multiple income streams?
- How do I build a team or automate?
- When/how do I transition full-time?
- How do I think about portfolio of ventures?
- What are sophisticated growth tactics?

**Ideal Episode Types:**
- Serial entrepreneurs with multiple successful exits
- Founders who scaled to 7+ figures
- VCs/investors with pattern recognition across many companies
- Operational experts (COOs, growth leaders)
- Platform builders and ecosystem creators
- Advanced strategy discussions (pricing, acquisition, exits)

**Learning Journey:**
1. Hiring, delegation, and team building
2. Systems and automation for scale
3. Multiple income streams & portfolio approach
4. Advanced growth tactics (paid acquisition, partnerships, etc.)
5. Transitioning from side hustle to full-time
6. Exit strategies, selling businesses, or long-term wealth building
