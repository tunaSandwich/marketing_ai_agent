# True Crime Podcast Recommendations

## Investigative Journalism
- **Serial** - The podcast that started it all, investigative journalism at its best
- **Criminal** - Short-form true crime by Phoebe Judge, beautifully produced
- **Bear Brook** - Deep dive into cold cases with meticulous research
- **In the Dark** - Exceptional investigative journalism, season 2 is particularly powerful

## Storytelling
- **S-Town** - Atmospheric storytelling from Serial team, unique narrative structure
- **Dr. Death** - Medical malpractice investigation, compelling and disturbing
- **Dirty John** - Psychological thriller format, well-crafted storytelling

## Discussion Style
- **Morbid** - Two friends discussing cases with humor and empathy
- **My Favorite Murder** - Comedy meets true crime, very popular format
- **True Crime Garage** - Detailed case discussions with beer reviews

## Cold Cases
- **Someone Knows Something** - Canadian investigative series
- **Your Own Backyard** - Student-led investigation into Kristin Smart case
- **The Trail Went Cold** - Focuses on unsolved mysteries

## International Cases
- **Casefile** - Australian true crime, excellent research and presentation
- **They Walk Among Us** - UK-based true crime podcast
