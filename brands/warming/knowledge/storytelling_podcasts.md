# Storytelling Podcast Recommendations

## Narrative Fiction
- **Welcome to Night Vale** - Surreal fictional community radio show
- **The Magnus Archives** - Horror fiction anthology with overarching plot
- **Wolf 359** - Science fiction audio drama set on a space station

## True Stories
- **This American Life** - Weekly stories on various themes
- **The Moth** - True stories told live on stage
- **StoryCorps** - Conversations between people who know each other

## Documentary Style
- **Heavyweight** - Jonathan Goldstein helps people resolve past issues
- **Reply All** - Internet culture stories with investigative elements
- **Crimetown** - Long-form investigative storytelling about crime

## Personal Narratives
- **Dear Sugars** - Advice column turned podcast with personal stories
- **Modern Love** - Essays about love and relationships
- **Terrible, Thanks for Asking** - Honest conversations about difficult topics

## Historical Narratives
- **Slow Burn** - Deep dives into major historical events
- **You Must Remember This** - Classic Hollywood history and secrets
- **Throughline** - How past events shape current issues

## Audio Drama
- **Limetown** - Mystery thriller about a missing research community
- **The Black Tapes** - Fictional paranormal investigation podcast
- **Alice Isn't Dead** - Horror road trip narrative
