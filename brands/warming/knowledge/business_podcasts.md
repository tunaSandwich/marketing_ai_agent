# Business Podcast Recommendations

## Entrepreneurship
- **How I Built This** - Stories behind famous companies and entrepreneurs
- **Masters of Scale** - Reid Hoffman interviews successful entrepreneurs
- **The Tim Ferriss Show** - Interviews with high performers across industries

## Investing & Finance
- **Chat with Traders** - Interviews with successful traders and investors
- **The Investors Podcast** - Value investing and market analysis
- **Planet Money** - Economic concepts explained in accessible ways

## Marketing & Sales
- **Marketing Over Coffee** - Marketing news and strategy discussions
- **Call to Action** - Conversion optimization and digital marketing
- **The GaryVee Audio Experience** - Gary Vaynerchuk's marketing insights

## Leadership & Management
- **HBR IdeaCast** - Harvard Business Review's management podcast
- **Stanford Leadership Podcast** - Leadership research and insights
- **Manager Tools** - Practical management advice and techniques

## Tech Business
- **a16z Podcast** - Andreessen Horowitz's tech and business discussions
- **The McKinsey Podcast** - Business strategy and consulting insights
