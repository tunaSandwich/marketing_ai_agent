# Comedy Podcast Recommendations

## Improvisational Comedy
- **Comedy Bang! Bang!** - Improvisational interviews with comedians and celebrities
- **improv4humans** - Improvisation based on audience suggestions
- **Spontaneanation** - Paul F. Tompkins leads improvised storytelling

## Interview Shows
- **WTF with Marc Maron** - In-depth interviews with comedians and celebrities
- **Conan O'Brien Needs a Friend** - Conan's conversational interview style
- **The Joe Rogan Experience** - Long-form conversations (comedy episodes)

## Panel Shows
- **The Dollop** - Two comedians discuss American history
- **My Dad Wrote A Porno** - Three friends read and critique amateur erotica
- **How Did This Get Made?** - Comedians discuss bad movies

## Storytelling
- **This American Life** - Often has comedic segments and stories
- **Risk!** - People tell true stories they never thought they'd share

## Daily/Weekly Shows
- **The Daily Zeitgeist** - Comedy news podcast
- **Pod Save America** - Political comedy/commentary
