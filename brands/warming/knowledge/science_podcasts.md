# Science Podcast Recommendations

## General Science
- **Science Vs** - Debunks myths and examines scientific claims
- **StarTalk** - Neil deGrasse Tyson's astrophysics and pop culture show
- **Science Friday** - Weekly science news and discussions

## Psychology & Neuroscience
- **Hidden Brain** - How unconscious patterns shape behavior
- **Brain Science** - Neuroscience research and discoveries
- **The Psychology Podcast** - Interviews with psychology researchers

## Technology & Innovation
- **Reply All** - Internet culture and technology stories
- **Radiolab** - Creative approach to science and philosophy topics
- **99% Invisible** - Design and architecture podcast

## Medical Science
- **The Nocturnists** - Medical storytelling from healthcare workers
- **Bedside Rounds** - Medical mysteries and case studies
- **The Naked Scientists** - Medical and health science topics

## Space & Astronomy
- **Planetary Radio** - Space exploration and astronomy
- **SpaceTime** - Weekly astronomy and space science news
- **The Space Show** - Interviews with space industry professionals

## Climate & Environment
- **Climate One** - Climate change discussions and solutions
- **Outside Podcast** - Environmental and outdoor adventure stories
