# History Podcast Recommendations

## Comprehensive History
- **Dan Carlin's Hardcore History** - Deep dives into historical events, exceptional storytelling
- **Revolutions** - Mike Duncan covers major revolutions throughout history
- **The History of Rome** - Comprehensive coverage of Roman history

## Military History
- **Battleground** - Military history podcast covering famous battles
- **War Stories** - Personal accounts from veterans and war correspondents
- **The History of WWII Podcast** - Detailed coverage of World War II

## Ancient History
- **The Ancient World** - Covers civilizations from ancient times
- **History of Ancient Greece** - Focused on Greek civilization
- **The Ancients** - Short episodes on ancient history topics

## Specific Topics
- **Stuff You Missed in History Class** - Covers overlooked historical stories
- **Lore** - Dark historical tales and folklore
- **Noble Blood** - Stories about royalty and nobility throughout history

## American History
- **American History Tellers** - Narrative-style American history
- **1865** - Weekly podcast covering events 150+ years ago
- **BackStory** - American history podcast with historian hosts
