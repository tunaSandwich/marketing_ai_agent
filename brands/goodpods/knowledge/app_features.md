# Goodpods App Features

## Core Features

### Social Discovery
- Follow friends and see what they're listening to
- Share your favorite episodes with your network
- Comment on episodes and create discussions
- Get recommendations based on your social graph

### Curated Collections
- Browse expertly curated podcast collections
- Discover new shows organized by theme, genre, or mood
- Access seasonal and trending collections
- Save collections for later listening

### Podcast Player
- Beautiful, intuitive interface designed for podcast listening
- Queue management for seamless episode transitions
- Variable playback speed controls
- Sleep timer and smart pause features
- Cross-device sync for listening continuity

### Import & Organization
- Easy import from Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and other apps
- Organize your subscriptions with custom tags and folders
- Smart episode recommendations based on listening history
- Automatic download management

### No Ads Promise
- Clean listening experience without interrupting ads
- Ad-free app interface and navigation
- Focus purely on content discovery and listening
- Premium experience available to all users for free

## Platform Availability
- iOS App Store
- Google Play Store
- Web player at goodpods.app
- Mobile-optimized experience across devices

## Discovery Engine
- AI-powered recommendations based on listening patterns
- Social signals from trusted friends and tastemakers
- Expert curation from podcast industry professionals
- Trending and popular content surfacing
